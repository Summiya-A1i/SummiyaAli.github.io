# Title: "Story 3: The Midterm Hustle 📑🎀"

### 🦋 Time Has Wings!
Time really does have wings when you're a fresher! 🦋 It felt like we had just walked through the UET gates a few days ago, and suddenly, the midterm schedule was staring us in the face. 🌪️ The realization hit us like a total shock—we were so caught up in the excitement of starting university life that we didn't realize how fast the weeks had slipped by! 🏛️✨ We immediately shifted into "survival mode," scrambling to collect notes, helping each other decode tough topics, and seeking every bit of wisdom from our seniors. 📑💖 That initial panic really brought us closer together as a batch! 🎀🌷

### 📉 The Solo Study Struggle & Midnight Noodles 
Midnight noodles became my ultimate survival fuel during midterm week! 🍜✨ As expected, Calculus and Applied Physics were the ultimate villains—truly a massive headache! 📉🙄 I quickly realized that I’m a total solo learner; while everyone else was group-studying, I found it hard to focus because I’d end up explaining things to my fellows instead of learning myself. 🎀 So, I spent my midterm prep tucked away in my room, surrounded by notes and the steam from my cup of noodles. 💅 Even though I had survived the quizzes, the actual "fear" of the first-ever university midterm was on a whole different level! 😱 It was a stressful solo journey, but it was the only way I could truly conquer those tough topics. 📖☕️🌷

### 🐍 Python: My Midterm "Main Character" Moment
While Python felt like a mountain to climb for most of my classmates, for me, it was the only midterm I actually looked forward to! ⌨️💕 Even without a single word of traditional *preparation, I felt so confident because I truly love the logic of coding. 💾✨ After practicing so many labs and tackling the real-world scenarios prepared by **Dr. Bilal*, the concepts felt like second nature to me. 🎀 Sitting in the lab and solving those Python puzzles was the highlight of my week—Dr. Bilal’s lab questions had prepared me so well that the exam didn't feel like a struggle, it felt like a passion I was finally getting to prove! 🐍👩‍💻🌷

> "When you love the logic, the exam becomes a playground." ✨💻🎀
