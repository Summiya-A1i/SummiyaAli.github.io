# Title: "Story 5: The Final Sprint 📑🌸"

### 🎆 The Final Countdown & Calm Vibes
As the year drew to a close, the New Year brought with it the reality of my first-ever university finals! 🎆✨ It was quite a shock, just like the midterms, especially since I was still in the middle of wrapping up major project milestones. 🏛️🌷 Between completing lab manuals, submitting a flurry of assignments, and catching up on post-midterm quizzes, the workload was definitely piling up. 📑🎀 However, thanks to my naturally calm personality, I managed to stay composed through it all. 🩰 I’ve never been the type to get stressed easily, so I handled every academic challenge with a steady pace and a clear mind. It felt like a period of focused growth rather than panic! 🌸🕊️

### 💻 The Last-Minute Logic & Printing Drama
Coding the Machine Learning project was actually the easy part! 💻✨ Since *Dr. Bilal* had already taught us the core logic, I felt confident tweaking the scripts and making the code my own. 💾🌸 The real drama, however, was the PF lab manual deadline. 📑😱 With the university printing shop closed for days, things got really tense right before the submission date! 🎢 It was a bit of a struggle to get everything printed and the project file finalized, but I managed to pull it all together with a fellow's help. 🎀 Even though I had to hustle for a couple of hours, I actually enjoyed the excitement of working against the clock! 🫣✨ Just when things were at the edge, Dr. Bilal kindly extended the deadline—it was a total lifesaver that allowed me to return to my usual "calm and collected" self. 🌷🩰🦢

### 🎂 Exhibition, Pride, and Cake!
For me, the project was about so much more than just writing code or applying Machine Learning—it was about finding my voice! 🎤✨ *Dr. Bilal* organized a project exhibition where we had to present our work, and it was such a massive confidence booster. 🌸 Having seniors and even my male classmates come over to ask questions about my model was actually really fun! 🎀 Explaining my logic to them helped me spot my own mistakes and understand my work even better. 💾✨ The best part was the big celebration afterward where Dr. Bilal cut a cake with both sections of Computer Engineering. 🎂🌷 Seeing how genuinely happy he was for our success made me feel so proud of myself. It was truly one of the most special moments of my university life so far! 🏛️ Swan 🦢💖
